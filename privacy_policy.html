Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 19, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Community Calendar ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

When you register for an account, we collect:

  • Username: Your chosen display name
  • Email Address: For account verification and communication
  • Password: Encrypted and stored securely
  • ZIP Code: Oklahoma ZIP codes (73000-74999) to show you relevant local events

2.2 Event Information

When you create or interact with events, we collect:

  • Event details (title, description, date, time, location)
  • RSVP responses
  • Favorite events
  • Events you create or manage

2.3 Technical Information

We automatically collect:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version
  • Usage statistics and analytics
  • Error logs and crash reports

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our service
  • Create and manage your account
  • Show you events relevant to your location (ZIP code)
  • Allow you to RSVP and interact with events
  • Send you important notifications about your account or events
  • Improve our app and user experience
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is:

  • Encrypted in transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses HTTPS encryption
  • Securely stored: Data is stored on MongoDB Atlas with industry-standard security measures
  • Password protected: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt encryption
  • Access controlled: Only authorized personnel can access user data

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Public event information: Events you create are visible to other users in your area
  • Service providers: We use MongoDB Atlas (database hosting) and Render.com (backend hosting)
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Data portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications

7. Data Retention

We retain your information:

  • As long as your account is active
  • For 90 days after account deletion (for recovery purposes)
  • Longer if required by law or for legitimate business purposes

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

9. Location Data

We only collect your ZIP code, not precise GPS location. ZIP code information is used solely to:

  • Show you events in your area
  • Ensure the app is used within Oklahoma
  • Improve local event recommendations

10. Cookies and Tracking

Our mobile app does not use cookies. We use secure authentication tokens to maintain your login session.

11. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • MongoDB Atlas: Database hosting (Privacy Policy: https://www.mongodb.com/legal/privacy-policy)
  • Render.com: Backend hosting (Privacy Policy: https://render.com/privacy)

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an email notification (for major changes)

13. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to exercise your rights, contact us:

  • Email: okkiegaming@gmail.com
  • Developer: OkkieGaming
  • Location: Blackwell, Oklahoma, USA

14. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect and how we use it.

15. International Users

This app is designed for users in Oklahoma, United States. If you access the app from outside the US, your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States.

16. Do Not Track

We do not track users across third-party websites. Our app does not respond to Do Not Track (DNT) signals.

17. User-Generated Content

Events you create are public and can be viewed by other users. Please do not include sensitive personal information in event descriptions.

18. Account Deletion

To delete your account:

  1. Open the app and go to Settings
  2. Select "Delete Account"
  3. Confirm deletion
  4. Or email us at okkiegaming@gmail.com with your username

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone after 90 days.

By using Community Calendar, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
